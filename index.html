Lakeside Coffee Bar in Burlington, VT

How you start your day is important. Why not start it with us? Roasted and Toasted Lakeside Coffee Bar is not your typical coffee shop. We create specialty, high-quality coffee blends for true coffee lovers. Since our founding in 1999, we've been committed to creating a relaxing experience to keep customers coming back. Whether it's our collection of hand-crafted pastries or our curated roasted beverages, we have what you need to start your morning, afternoon, or evening the "roasted and toasted" way.
Don't forget to ask our barista for the seasonal specials!

Our Story

Roasted and Toasted Lakeside Coffee Bar is a locally owned coffee shop by a beautiful lakeside in Burlington, Vermont. We offer a range of signature coffee-based beverages and hand-crafted pastries and breakfast sandwiches. Since our humble beginnings in 1999, our goal has been to create a relaxing, refreshing environment for those who love coffee like we do!.
