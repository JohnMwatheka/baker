Our Story

Roasted & Toasted Lakeside Coffee Bar is a locally owned coffee shop in beautiful Burlington, Vermont.

Since our humble beginnings in 1999, our goal has been to create a relaxing, refreshing environment for coffee lovers. We offer signature coffee-based beverages and hand-crafted pastries and breakfast sandwiches, all made with locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Our lakeside location provides the perfect backdrop for enjoying your favorite brew while taking in views of Lake Champlain.
